GLU-CAM

It is necessary to know the blood Glucose level

GluCam: an economical,webapp-based, autonomous blood glucose monitoring and diabetes sensing application.

Diabetes is a medical condition in which the body cannot produce or effectively convert blood glucose into the required energy leads. Glucose is a primary energy source required for proper body functioning and transported to the individual cells via the bloodstream. When the blood glucose level persistently remains above the nominal value, diabetes develops.

Features of site

ANALYSIS

An autonomous, optical-based, smartphone implemented blood glucose measurement and diabetes sensing model.

Smart Healthcare

A Smart-Healthcare architecture for diabetes diagnosis.

Glucose Prediction

The proposed smartphone based gluCam model can:
Extraction : Automatically extract the blood vessels as ROI from the digitally captured eye image.
Prediction : Accurately and non-invasively predict diabetes and blood glucose without incursion of external gadgets.
Better :Outperform existing similar models in terms of accuracy, sensitivity and specificity.
Robust : Make the prediction accuracy robust to light and device.

HOW TO USE

The web-application predicts the glucose level from the eye images input given.
